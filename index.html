Garden Design

At Lucky Shrub, we are passionate about creating breathtaking outdoor spaces that capture the essence of natural beauty. Our garden design services are led by our skilled garden architect, Jason. With a keen eye for detail and a deep love for plants, Jason and his team craft enchanting gardens that transform any space into a captivating oasis. Whether you dream of a tranquil retreat, a vibrant paradise, or a modern landscape, our experts will work closely with you to turn your vision into a reality. From initial design concepts to the finishing touches, Lucky Shrub is here to bring your dream garden to life.

Plant Nursery

Explore our carefully curated selection of indoor and outdoor plants at Lucky Shrub's plant nursery. Led by Maria, our nursery offers a wide range of plant varieties, from lush houseplants to resilient outdoor blooms. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a novice plant enthusiast, our friendly team is here to assist you in choosing the perfect plants.

About Us

Lucky Shrub is a family-owned garden design and plant nursery firm located in beautiful Tucson, Arizona. Founded by the husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, our story is rooted in a deep and shared passion for all things green. Jason, our garden architect, leads our talented landscaping team, creating stunning garden designs that reflect his expertise and creative vision. Maria oversees the marketing and operation of our thriving plant nursery, ensuring that our customers have access to a diverse range of top-quality plants. Together, we're committed to turning your outdoor dreams into reality and providing you with the very best in plant selection and care. At Lucky Shrub, we're dedicated to transforming spaces into cherished oases you can be proud of.
